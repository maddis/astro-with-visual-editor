What you get being a pet sitter with us.

Wonderful animals

enjoy the benefits of having a dog without the costs and full time commitments

Flexible schedule

look after some amazing pets on a schedule that suits you

Fun, expert training

you will become certified in our animal care courses

Welfare-first

join our movement to provide welfare- first pet care across the UK

Expenses paid

Meet Sarah, pet sitter at We Love Pets.

Already being a dog walker, the opportunity came up for being a pet sitter and because I have a dog of my own and he loves other animals, I thought it was the perfect opportunity to open up my home and gain a little bit of extra money for my family. 

We get to enjoy days out, like going to the beach and the park and if you dont have a pet of your own, to look after them, without the full time commitment of owning a pet, can brighten up your day

How do I become a pet sitter

Apply online

Apply for becoming a dog walker through our easy application here. Fill in some quick details to find out more about becoming a pet sitter.

Meet for a chat

Have a chat and a coffee with your local We Love Pets branch owner to find out more about the branch and the role.

Well check your house

The branch owner will come to your house just to ensure its ready to home some lovely dogs! A garden is essential so dogs have outside space.

Youre ready to go

Now youre ready to start looking after some wonderful pets! Youll have everything you need to begin bookings on your schedule.

Become pet certified

Its time to become a certified pet sitter by completing our animal care courses, including pet first aid..

Meet some dogs

Its time to go out and meet some of the lovely dogs youll be looking after. Go on a walk with the branch owner and see how you get on with the dogs.
